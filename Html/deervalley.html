Deer Valley

Deer Valley is know for it's luxurios ski lifts, perfectly groomed runs, and exceptional customer service. It is one of the two resorts in Utah that prohibits snowboarding. Not only does it prohibit snowboarding but also any other activities during the summer that could cause the ground to be torn up for ski season.

Pros and Cons of Deer Valley:

  1. PROS
    • It is the most luxurious ski resort of Utah
    • Offers perfect terrain for intermediates
    • Family oriented and has a highly regarded ski school
  2. CONS
    • Even though the lift ticket sales are supposedly limited each day, the place gets crowded, and the slopes that are groomed can get congested
    • Very expensive, it is the most expensive resort in Utah
    • Probably isn’t the best place to go if you’re a hard-core expert skier.
