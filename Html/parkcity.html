Park City Mountain Resort

The skiing and snowboarding here is world-class with two combined resorts and a state-of-the-art action sports hub just a few miles apart. Park City Mountain, with its current purchase of Canyons Ski Resort is now the largest ski resort in the country.

Pros and Cons of Park City Mountain Resort:

  1. PROS
    • Park City Utah skiing is massive, especially now with the interconnection with the former Canyons Resort to form one large ski area with 7,300 acres of skiable terrain.
    • The lift infrastructure is of high quality.
    • Has phenomenal intermediate groomed runs and great snow quality.
    • Well renowned as being great for families, but others will enjoy it equally. It's a great all-rounder
  2. CONS
    • Expert skiers may not find as challenging terrain at Park City as they would at Alta, Snowbird or Solitude.
    • Very expensive compare to the other Utah Ski resorts
